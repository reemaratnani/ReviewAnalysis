Employee Reviews Analysis

The purpose of this project is to compare the reviews of 6 corporations: Facebook, Google, Apple, Amazon, Microsoft and Netflix from an employee's perspective. This dataset contains web scraped data for over 67k employee reviews. The plots included herein are based on employee's opinion about several aspects related to employment by company. These companies are compared based on the overall ratings, career opportunities, work-life balance, management, culture values and benefits ratings.
